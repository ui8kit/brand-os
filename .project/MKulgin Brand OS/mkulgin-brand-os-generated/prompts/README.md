# MKulgin Brand OS Prompt Index

These files are isolated prompts for starting surface-specific application work with the brand schema attached as the source-of-truth contract.

- `landing.md` — Introduce Maksim Kulgin as the central node of a tech-media hub: journal, community club, catalog, and ecosystem. Hero with portrait and thesis, path timeline, proof, and clear CTAs.
- `tech-blog.md` — List essays, case studies, and operator notes with editorial rhythm and strong topical categorization.
- `article-detail.md` — Present long-form technical or business writing with high readability, clear metadata, and evidence-oriented callouts.
- `about.md` — Explain the biography, values, and working philosophy behind the brand without hype or self-mythologizing.
- `library.md` — Present books, engineering foundations, and intellectual capital as part of the brand's trust layer.
- `projects.md` — Show products, services, and business cases as evidence of operating experience rather than startup theater.
- `media.md` — Collect videos, interviews, external media, and platform presence into a coherent proof surface.
- `newsletter.md` — Convert readers into subscribers through clear value exchange and low-noise trust signals.
- `community.md` — Club space for systematic people: topic channels, weekly digest, pinned threads, archivable discussions. Not a chat clone — a smart club space where discussions can be read and found.
- `catalog.md` — Curated B2B catalog of community participants: services, products, teams, studios, experts, founders. A trusted professional network showcase.
- `grants.md` — How to prepare applications, real mistakes, win/loss stories, fund breakdowns, materials for founders, templates and checklists.
- `events.md` — Announcements, AMA, online/offline meetings, community gatherings.
- `archive.md` — Long-range credibility: internet of 90s and 2000s, network protocols, how e-commerce changed, lessons from old systems for new startups.
